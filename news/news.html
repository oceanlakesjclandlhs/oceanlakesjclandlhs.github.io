State Convention


By Webmaster
Posted November 28, 2014

State convention was this past weekend, and it was an absolute blast! OLHS was well represented with 18 students and 2 chaperones making the journey to Richmond. OLHS JCL would also like to extend a hearty congratulations to fellow Virgina-Beach-resident and PAHS delegation member, Andrew Zazzera, for winning the office of VJCL State Parliamentarian! Be sure to keep an eye on the website for some photos from convention, as well as future updates. In the meanwhile, have a great Thanksgiving!
