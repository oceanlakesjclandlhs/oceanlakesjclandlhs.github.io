Useful Links


  • National Junior Classical League Website

The official website of the National Junior Classical League. This site is an excellent place to learn more about the JCL, what it does, and how to get involved on a national level, in addition to information about the National convention.

  • Virginia Junior Classical League Website

The official website of the Virginia Junior Classical League. This site has information about the state convention, in addition to info about Certamen, academics, and other state events.

  • Ocean Lakes High School Website

The official website of Ocean Lakes High School. Here, one can learn about events at the school, its academic programs, and other information.

  • William Whitaker's Words

Extremely comprehensive Latin dictionary. WWW is able to define and decline about 39,000 different latin words, and also translate English words into Latin.

  • The Latin Library

Collection of numerous Latin authors, ranging from Ammianus to Vitruvius.

  • MIT Classics Library

MIT's collection of classical translations. Includes both Greek and Roman authors.

  • Digital Atlas of Ancient Rome

Extraordinarily accurate map of Ancient Rome, able to be navigated in a style much like Google Maps.
