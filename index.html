Who are we?

Ocean Lakes High School

Ocean Lakes High School is a comprehensive secondary school program located in Virginia Beach, Virginia. Coupled with the Math and Science Academy, also located at the school, OLHS has a population of about 2,500 students. Famed for its successful sports programs in addition to its high quality academic programs, Ocean Lakes provides an excellent atmosphere for learning.

Junior Classical League

The National Junior Classical League was established in 1936 to promote interest in the Greek and Latin languages, in addition to the culture and history associated with those languages. The Ocean Lakes High School JCL chapter aims to accomplish this through organizing events and activities to promote and educate others about Latin and the Classical Antiquity.

Latin Honor Society

The Latin Honor Society is an organization within the JCL, which designates students who have exemplified continued academic profficency in Latin. The Ocean Lakes LHS chapter meets concurrently with the OLHS JCL chapter.
